    🚀 What & Who is PGSVerse?

    PGSVerse is not an admin, it’s not a teacher — it’s us, the students of Prachin Global School. We’re among you. Could be your classmate, your best friend, even the guy who copies from you 😜

    This site was built for fun, chaos, and creativity — by students, for students.

    🎯 What’s the Point?

    PGSVerse is a full-on digital version of school fun — gossip, roast battles, events, flex, files, badges, leaderboards. Basically: entertainment + clout.

    📲 How to Get Started
    • Sign in using your Google account
    • Fill in your profile: name, class, role
    • You’ll land on your Dashboard where you’ll see your:
      • Name & Email
      • Role + Class
      • 🪙 VCoins
      • 🎖️ Badges
    🪙 What Are VCoins?

    VCoins are like your digital reputation points. Earn them by doing cool stuff and flex on the leaderboard. In future, you can redeem badges, NFTs, even merch (maybe 👀).

    • +100 for gossip
    • +50 for event wins
    • Daily bonuses, welcome gift, profile fill etc.
    🎖️ What Are Badges?

    Badges = flex items. You can earn special ones like:

    • “Gossip Master” after 10 worthy gossips
    • “Note Wizard” for top uploads
    • “Lounge Elite” for VIP access
    🕵️ How to Use GossipVerse
    • Go to GossipVerse
    • Type your gossip anonymously
    • Click "Send Gossip"
    • Earn +100 VCoins instantly if it gets accepted
    • Be spicy, not boring 😉
    🗨️ How TalkVerse Works

    TalkVerse = discussion threads. Admin starts the thread. You reply, debate, vibe, roast, confess, anything.

    Fun replies may earn VCoins. LoungeVerse has TalkVerse too, with 5x coin multiplier.
